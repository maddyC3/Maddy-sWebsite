Coral tree maintenance

"Coral trees" are tree-like structures built out of PVC pipes with a buoy at the top to float at the water's surface.
Generally, the Coral Trees are around 15 feet tall in 15 foot deep water. The Tree has branches, made of PVC pipes,
that stick out and have baby coral hanging off of it by a special string

Overtime, algae builds up on the branches of coral and prevents the coral from photosynthesizing.
To prevent this, divers use many techniques to clean off the algae.

  1. Metal brushes
  2. Push pins
  3. Toothbrushes
  4. ETC
